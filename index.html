This website is about cows

I absolutely LOVE cows. This website has been built to share some wonderful information about these lovely, fuzzy, funny creatures. It also has some photos. I really do love cows, and hopefully after viewing all this you will moo.

Let me tell you about cows.

This little guy is such fluffy. Fluffy cows are great for cold winter nights. They always keep you warm.

Sometimes cows will roll in gangs to intimidate tufts of grass. That cold hard stare has my milk shakin'.

These whiskers are bigger than most cats. They help the cows to sense out the tastiest morsels of grass around.

This is not actually a photo of a cow, this is a pig. You will find pigs on farms just like cows.
